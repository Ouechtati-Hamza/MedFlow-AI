# MedFlow AI 🏥🤖

## AI-Powered Production Management Platform

MedFlow AI is an intelligent production management platform designed to transform traditional paper-based processes into automated, data-driven workflows.

Developed during **Tunisia Global Forum 2026 AI Hackathon**, the project reached the **Top 4 teams**.

---

## 🚀 Problem

Many production environments still rely on manual data recording, making it difficult to monitor performance, identify inefficiencies, and make fast decisions.

---

## 💡 Solution

MedFlow AI provides:

- Automated production data collection
- Centralized information management
- AI-powered analysis
- Efficiency improvement insights
- Better decision-making support
